ODRS

Open Age Ratings Service

The Open Age Ratings Service allows free and commercial software to generate the OARS XML required for the AppData file. OARS relies on honest answers from upstream projects and is purely informational. The original set of questions was been inspired by the now-defunct TIGRS and the mapping from rating to minimum suggested age data comes from common sense.
